JUDGES

The National Show itself is on Saturday, May 2nd, 2015 and our judge will be Travis Finkle.

 

The Ohio State Rabbit Breeders Association will host a Britannia Petite Specialty Show in addition on this same day and our judge will be Paul Kyle.

 

There will be a Single Show for Brits  in OSRBA's All Breed Show on Sunday, May 3rd, 2015.

 

So Brits can be shown a total of 3 times this weekend!
